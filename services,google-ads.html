Google Ads

1. What are Google Ads?

Google Ads is an online advertising platform that allows businesses to show their products and services directly to people searching on Google. It works on a pay-per-click (PPC) model, meaning you only pay when someone clicks your ad. With Google Ads, you can target customers by keywords, location, interests, and even demographics, ensuring your message reaches the right audience at the right time.

2. What Happens If You Don’t Use Google Ads?

Without Google Ads, your business may miss out on thousands of potential customers who are actively searching for what you offer. Relying only on organic traffic can be slow and competitive, making it hard to stay visible in front of your target audience. Competitors using Google Ads will appear above you in search results, which can lead to lost sales, fewer leads, and weaker brand recognition.
