1. What is Social Media Marketing?

Social Media Marketing (SMM) is the process of promoting your business, products, or services on social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, and TikTok. By creating engaging content, running ads, and interacting with followers, SMM helps businesses increase brand awareness, reach the right audience, and build customer loyalty.

2. What Happens If You Don’t Use Social Media Marketing?

Without social media marketing, your brand may lose visibility in a world where millions of users spend hours daily on these platforms. Competitors leveraging SMM can capture the attention of your potential customers, reducing your reach and engagement. Organic growth alone can be slow, and missing out on social media opportunities can mean fewer leads, reduced website traffic, and lower sales.
